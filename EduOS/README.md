# EduOS – AI-Powered Personalized Learning Platform

## Problem Statement

Modern education often suffers from **fragmented learning systems**, where students rely on multiple platforms like **YouTube, Coursera, books, and tutorials**. This leads to several challenges:

- **Lack of personalized learning path:** Students struggle to identify which concepts to learn first.
- **Difficulty identifying knowledge gaps:** Weak areas are often overlooked, causing inefficient learning.
- **Information overload:** With too many resources available, students can get overwhelmed.
- **No centralized tracking:** Progress, strengths, and weaknesses are scattered across platforms.

As a result, students frequently **spend time learning the wrong things** or **learn inefficiently**, reducing overall effectiveness.

---

## EduOS – Our Solution

EduOS is an **AI-powered learning system** designed to address these challenges and provide **personalized, structured, and adaptive learning**. Key innovations include:

### 1. AI Learning Roadmap Generator
- Students enter a goal, e.g., “Become a Data Scientist”.
- The AI generates a **structured 8-week roadmap**, including focus skills, topics, and practice tasks.

### 2. Skill Gap Detection Engine
- Tracks student performance on quizzes and exercises.
- Automatically identifies **weak concepts** and highlights areas needing improvement.

### 3. Adaptive Learning System
- Dynamically updates the learning roadmap based on student **progress and performance**.
- Ensures students focus on areas where they need the most improvement.

### 4. AI Learning Copilot
- Acts as a **personal tutor** within the platform.
- Students can **ask questions** and receive **instant explanations** or guidance on concepts.

### 5. Unified Learning Dashboard
- Central hub to **track learning progress, strengths, and improvement areas**.
- Provides visual insights to help students **stay on track** and **optimize their learning**.
